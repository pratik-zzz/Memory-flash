How to play

1. The computer randomly flashes tiles of different colors in a sequence.

2. You need to flash (click) the tiles in the same sequence as the computer.

3. If you match the sequence correctly, your score increases, and the computer flashes a new, longer sequence.

4. If you make a mistake, your score resets to 0 and the game starts over.

5. The game does not end, it's all about how far you can go. Enjoy!
